The P.A.I.D. Framework

A four-part checklist that removes guessing and replaces it with qualified decisions

P
Position

Trade with the 200 EMA. Above = buy bias. Below = sell bias.

A
Area

Trade meaningful locations. Liquidity zones, equal highs/lows, Opening Range.

I
Indicator

Wait for confirmation. EMA 9 and EMA 50 alignment proves momentum.

D
Direction

React to structure. BOS, CHoCH, and breakouts confirm the move.
